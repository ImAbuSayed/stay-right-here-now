[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the embers glowing in the dark
Your gentle breathing leaves a tender mark
Don't let this midnight magic fade away
Tonight I only need for you to stay

[Verse 1]
The world outside is spinning out of sight
It's only you and me beneath this light
I trace the shadows falling on your face
And lose myself inside this secret place

[Pre-Chorus]
The hours blur, the fire burns so slow
There's nowhere else that we could ever go
I pull you close and whisper in your ear
That everything I ever want is here

[Chorus]
Stay right here now, don't let the morning in
I want to feel the warmth upon your skin
Stay right here now, and let the shadows fall
Tonight I want to give to you my all

Don't let the morning in, just let it be
